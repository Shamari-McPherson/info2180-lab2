Buying Our Starter Home

September 29, 2024 by Shamari McPherson

Mean Green Or Too Much Green

Buying your first home is a major milestone, but it can seem daunting, especially if you're on a tight budget. In The Sims 4, even aspiring homeowners can achieve their dreams with a little creativity and planning. With careful budgeting and savvy shopping, you can find a charming starter home for under 20k Simoleons.

Explore quaint neighborhoods, bargain hunt at local auctions, or even consider renovating a fixer-upper to create your perfect cozy haven. Remember, the most important thing is to find a space that reflects your Sim's personality and provides a solid foundation for future growth.

But did I make it too green?

Sleep Is Never Enough

October 1, 2024 by Shamari McPherson

When Do I Sleep?

Barn Land has become my second home, a sprawling wilderness filled with endless possibilities. But as I trudge through the muddy fields, searching for hidden bushes and random scraps to sell for a quick buck, my Sim's energy levels are taking a serious hit.

Sleepless nights have become the norm, as I desperately try to squeeze in a few hours of rest between scavenging expeditions. Maybe it's time to invest in a comfy bed or a reliable alarm clock. Or perhaps I should just take a break from the grind and enjoy the simple pleasures of Sim life.

Do the poor not deserve sleep?

About

Welcome to my Sims 4 blog! Join me as I embark on a challenging yet rewarding journey with my Sim. Starting with absolutely no Simoleons, we're determined to build a thriving barn from scratch. Follow along as we overcome obstacles, discover new opportunities, and experience the joys and frustrations of rural living. From planting crops and tending to animals to building relationships with neighbors and participating in community events, we'll share our experiences and offer tips for aspiring Sim farmers. Let's see if we can turn this dream into a reality!
